Information about us

Learning a new language starts with building a strong vocabulary. Whether you are starting from scratch or looking to expand your knowledge, EveryDay English provides the ultimate tools to help you memorize words efficiently and permanently. We combine proven study techniques with interactive testing to turn your language goals into reality.

Learning Mode

Discover new vocabulary in a stress-free environment. In our Learning Mode, you can explore curated word lists, listen to native pronunciations, and see how words are used in real-life contexts. It is designed to help your brain absorb and understand new information naturally at your own pace.

Control Mode

Ready to challenge yourself? Switch to our Control Mode to put your memory to the test. Through engaging quizzes, spelling exercises, and flashcards, you can evaluate your retention, identify weak spots, and solidify your active vocabulary.
